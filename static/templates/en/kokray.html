Enstiti Kreyòl Entènasyonal

Enstiti Kreyòl Entènasyonal la se yon pwojè ki genyen pwomosyon lang kreyòl la pou bi. Li vle fè pwomosyon lang kreyòl la an Ayiti, epi nan tout mond lan. Li ap fè sa a travè kreyasyon kòpis lang nan, diksyonè nan lang nan epi li ap itlize lang nan pwodiksyon nan sijè akademik e syantifik.

The Plan

The plan of the Institute at the moment is to support the advancement of the project, which includes, research, team building, developmental planning, as well as the diffusion of these information. There is much work to be done, however, together, we can make it happen.
Further the development of our language is furthering our country!

How can you Help!

You can support this project of furthering the usage of the language in multiple ways. You can help diffuse this information to schools, you can become a delegate for the project, and you can become a member of the Institute, working directly on the project. By working on the project, you can provide support in compiling, and/or translating documents, or provide support on a specific field of study. Last but not least, you can sponsor this project, which in return will help the Haitian community as a whole.

The Research

We are currently looking for students of all fields of study to use the language in research, as well as publications. We are particularly focusing on the usage of the language in the scientific fields; however, we welcome all fields. We are also looking for researchers who can help in the development of the corpus, as well as the monolingual Ecological and Environmental Science dictionary in Haitian Creole. Furthermore, we are looking for experts in the agricultural and linguistic sciences to help further the project, or to do research in those fields.
